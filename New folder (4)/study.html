Real-world knowledge for real lives

However you choose to study with us, we put you right at the centre of the learning experience.

That means regardless of where you live or what pace best suits your lifestyle, we have a programme that will equip you with practical knowledge designed to be put into action. It’s not just about end results though. Our team will be here throughout your studies, ready to offer all the support you need to help make the most of your Edinburgh Business School journey. And, as one of our alumni, you will also have lifelong access to our exclusive resources, expertise and network.

How do you want to study?

There are many ways to learn with Edinburgh Business School.

Study in your home. Attend classes full- or part-time in Edinburgh. Or join your fellow students at a partner institution in your home country. You can stick to one route or combine them. It’s up to you.
